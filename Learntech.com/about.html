Who We Are

LearnTech is a leading e-learning platform in Africa dedicated to offering accessible, affordable, and high-quality tech education. We believe the future is digital and everyone deserves the opportunity to learn modern skills to thrive in this fast-changing world.

Our Mission

To democratize access to technology education through innovative, self-paced online courses built by industry professionals.

Our Vision

Empower 10 million African youth with job-ready digital skills by 2030. LearnTech provides a platform for students, job seekers, entrepreneurs, and anyone curious about tech to build a better future.

Our Values

  • Accessibility
  • Affordability
  • Relevance
  • Community
  • Innovation
